Close Protection (CPO)

Invisible, hyper-alert, and highly trained. The Vanguard physical detail.

Operational Definition

Close Protection goes far beyond physical guarding. Vanguard CPOs are intelligence-driven operatives. They do not stand out; they assimilate into the client's environment, dressed perfectly for the occasion—whether a corporate boardroom or a private yacht.

Every detail operative is trained in tactical medical response, advanced evasive driving techniques, and de-escalation psychology. Firearms are a last resort; intelligence is the primary weapon.

Core Training Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years Special Operations or Tier 1 Law Enforcement
  • Advanced Trauma Life Support Certification
  • Unarmed hand-to-hand combat proficiency
  • Extreme psychological stress inoculations

Availability is strictly limited. New clients must pass a prerequisite background check.
